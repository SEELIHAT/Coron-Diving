About the Course

The PADI Advanced Open Water Diver course helps you build confidence and expand your scuba skills through different adventure dives. It's designed to improve your diving techniques while exploring new underwater environments.

"The **PADI Advanced Open Water Diver Course**
is the next step in your scuba diving journey, designed to enhance your skills , expand your knowledge, and boost your confidence underwater. This course is perfect for certified Open Water Divers who want to explore new diving adventures, learn advanced techniques, and dive deeper.

#### **What You’ll Learn** The Advanced Open Water Diver Course is structured around five exciting adventure dives, including two mandatory dives and three elective dives of your choice. 1. **Mandatory Dives** - **Deep Dive**: Learn to dive safely to depths of up to 30 meters (100 feet) and understand the effects of pressure and nitrogen narcosis. - **Underwater Navigation Dive**: Master the use of a compass and natural navigation techniques to find your way underwater confidently. 2. **Elective Dives** Choose three additional dives based on your interests and goals, such as: - **Night Diving**: Experience the magic of diving in the dark with flashlights, discovering nocturnal marine life. - **Wreck Diving**: Explore sunken ships and artificial reefs, learning the basics of wreck exploration. - **Peak Performance Buoyancy**: Improve your buoyancy control for better air consumption and effortless movement. - **Drift Diving**: Learn to navigate and enjoy the thrill of diving with currents. - **Underwater Photography or Videography**: Capture stunning underwater moments. --- #### **Why Take This Course?** - **Skill Development**: Gain new diving skills and expand your comfort zone underwater. - **Explore Deeper**: Unlock access to deeper dive sites and more challenging environments. - **Adventure and Fun**: Try new types of diving tailored to your interests, from wrecks to night dives. - **Global Recognition**: An Advanced Open Water certification is recognized worldwide, allowing you to access a broader range of dive sites.

What You Will Learn

  • Deep diving techniques and safety procedures
  • Underwater navigation using a compass
  • Wreck diving and night diving experiences
  • Buoyancy control and underwater photography

Course Structure

The PADI Advanced Open Water Diver course consists of:

  1. Five adventure dives, including deep and navigation dives
  2. Theory sessions to understand dive planning and safety
  3. Optional specialty dives like wreck or night diving
