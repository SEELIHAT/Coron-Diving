About the Course

The PADI Scuba Review course is designed for certified divers who haven't been diving for a while and want to refresh their skills. This quick and easy refresher program will get you comfortable in the water again.

PADI ReActivate (Review) Course The PADI ReActivate Course is designed for certified divers who want to refresh their knowledge and skills after a period of inactivity. This program helps you regain confidence and ensures you are prepared for future dives.

What You’ll Learn Knowledge Review Refresh essential diving concepts, including safety procedures, dive planning, and emergency protocols. Study online through PADI eLearning or with an instructor. Confined Water Practice Review key scuba skills in a controlled environment, such as mask clearing, buoyancy control, and regulator recovery. Optional Open Water Dive Apply refreshed skills in an open water setting under the supervision of a PADI professional. Receive a ReActivate recognition card if required.

What You Will Refresh

  • Essential scuba diving safety procedures
  • Proper use of scuba gear
  • Underwater communication and hand signals
  • Basic diving techniques and buoyancy control

Course Structure

The PADI Scuba Review course includes:

  1. Knowledge Refresher (Online or Classroom)
  2. Confined Water Session to practice key skills
  3. Optional Open Water Dive to regain confidence

Course Pricing

The course fee is $150, which includes:

  • Instructor-led refresher training
  • Equipment rental
  • PADI Scuba Review certificate
