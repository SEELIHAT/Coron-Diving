### PADI Scuba Diver Course The **PADI Scuba Diver Course** is the perfect choice for those who want to experience the thrill of scuba diving but have limited time for a full certification. As an entry-level program, it allows you to dive under the direct supervision of a PADI professional to a maximum depth of 12 meters (40 feet). This course is a great stepping stone toward becoming a certified Open Water Diver. --- #### **What You’ll Learn** The course includes three essential components designed to introduce you to the basics of scuba diving: 1. **Knowledge Development** - Learn the fundamentals of scuba diving, including underwater communication, equipment use, and safety practices. - Study key concepts either in-person or online through PADI eLearning materials. 2. **Confined Water Dives** - Practice basic skills in a controlled environment, such as a pool or shallow water. - Skills include mask clearing, regulator recovery, and buoyancy control. 3. **Open Water Dives** - Experience the excitement of diving in open water under the direct supervision of a PADI professional. - Complete two dives to explore the underwater world while applying the skills you’ve learned. --- #### **Why Choose This Course?** - **Quick and Flexible**: Ideal for those with limited time, the course can be completed in just 1–2 days. - **Discover the Underwater World**: Gain a safe and enjoyable introduction to scuba diving. - **Pathway to Certification**: Easily upgrade to the PADI Open Water Diver certification in the future by completing additional training. --- #### **Course Requirements** - **Age**: Minimum age is 10 years. - **Health**: Participants must be in good physical health and comfortable in the water. - **Swimming Skills**: Basic swimming ability is required. --- #### **What’s Included** - PADI Scuba Diver manual or eLearning materials. - Full scuba equipment rental, including mask, fins, regulator, and BCD. - Certified instructor guidance throughout the course. - PADI Scuba Diver certification upon successful completion. --- #### **Duration and Cost** - The course typically takes 1–2 days to complete, depending on your schedule. - Costs range from $200 to $400, including training materials, equipment rental, and certification fees. --- #### **Why Choose Us?** At our dive center, we focus on creating a safe, enjoyable, and personalized experience for every student.
