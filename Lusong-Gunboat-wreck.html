Your Journey to the wreck diving

About the Lusong Gunboat Wreck

The Lusong Gunboat Wreck is a small Japanese warship sunk during World War II. Resting at shallow depths of 5 to 14 meters, it is one of the most accessible wrecks in Coron, Palawan. Perfect for snorkelers and beginner divers, the Lusong Gunboat Wreck is famous for its vibrant marine life and rich coral growth.

What Makes It Special

Unlike deeper wrecks, the Lusong Gunboat Wreck is bathed in sunlight, creating a stunning underwater scene. Its shallow location allows snorkelers to enjoy a close-up view of the wreck without diving equipment. The ship is now a thriving reef, home to colorful corals, anemones, and schools of fish. This wreck combines history, accessibility, and natural beauty, making it a must-visit site.

Highlights of Diving the Lusong Gunboat

  • Depth: 5-14 meters
  • Visibility: 10-20 meters
  • Marine Life: Reef fish, corals, anemones, and occasional rays
  • Unique Features: Sunlit wreck with vibrant coral growth
  • Experience Level: Ideal for snorkelers, beginner, and intermediate divers

Dive Tips for the Lusong Gunboat Wreck

The Lusong Gunboat is a beginner-friendly dive site, but its beauty can be equally enjoyed by snorkelers. Be cautious of currents during your dive, and always carry a dive light to explore the interior details of the wreck. It's best to dive during midday to take advantage of optimal sunlight.

Planning Your Dive

This wreck is easily accessible via boat tours from Coron, which cater to both divers and snorkelers. Many operators combine this site with nearby attractions like Lusong Coral Garden, making for a memorable day trip. The dry season from November to May offers the best visibility and calmer waters.

Additional Information

The Lusong Gunboat Wreck is a favorite for underwater photography due to its shallow depth and sunlit surroundings. The combination of vibrant marine life, historical value, and ease of access makes it a perfect choice for families, beginner divers, and those looking for a relaxing snorkeling experience.
