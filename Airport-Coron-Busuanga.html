Francisco B. Reyes Airport

About the Airport

Busuanga Airport, officially called Francisco B. Reyes Airport, is the main airport serving Coron, Palawan. It is located on Busuanga Island, around 30-40 minutes away from Coron town by land. Flights arrive here daily from Manila and Cebu via Philippine Airlines, Cebu Pacific, and Sunlight Air.

The airport is small and easy to navigate. There are a few souvenir stands and a waiting area.

Getting to Coron Town

Outside the airport, you will find vans waiting for passengers going to Coron town. This transport is called a “minivan” or “airport shuttle.” It is the easiest way to go to your hotel. You can book it in advance or just pay at the spot. The usual price is 150–200 pesos per person.

Vans go directly to most hotels or can drop you near the town center. Travel time is about 30–40 minutes depending on traffic and road condition. If you need to go back to the airport from your hotel, you can ask your hotel to book the same kind of shuttle van.

Tips

  • Bring cash – no ATM or big store at the airport
  • Keep your plane ticket and ID ready – sometimes checked again outside
  • Here to easy find vans to hotel and from hotel also ask at the reception
