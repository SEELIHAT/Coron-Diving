Lualhati Park or local Harbor

About Lualhati Park

Lualhati Park is the central harbor and public waterfront space in Coron Town, Palawan. It functions as the main jump-off point for most island hopping and boat tours around Coron Bay and the nearby islands.
The park itself offers scenic views of the bay and is often used by locals and tourists for leisure, jogging, or relaxing by the water. It's also a common gathering point in the early morning before tours begin.
You can always get an inexpensive snack there. Mno -frills Lots of simple cafes without fancy decor, but with tasty, home-cooked food at reasonable prices
There is a market nearby where you can buy fresh fruits, vegetables and meat at affordable prices.

Harbor Highlights

Lualhati Park serves as a local harbor where dozens of boats are docked daily. From here, travelers can catch boats to a wide range of destinations:

  • Island Hopping: Tours to Kayangan Lake, Twin Lagoon, Siete Pecados, and Skeleton Wreck
  • Diving Trips: Departures to WWII wreck sites and coral gardens
  • Private Boat Rentals: Custom island adventures with flexible schedules
  • Ferry Transfers: Some ferries to nearby islands like Culion and Linapacan

The harbor area is well-organized, with tour operators, snack vendors, and tricycle parking nearby. While it's a functional transport hub, it also retains a relaxing, local vibe, especially at sunset.
