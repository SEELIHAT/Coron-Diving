About Marine Park, Coron Palawan

Marine Park is a special protected area in Coron, Palawan, famous for its clear waters and rich underwater life. For will see something you must buy tiket on the boat is 350 pesos per person. Located close to Coron town, it is easily accessible by boat and popular among snorkelers and divers. This area is full of colorful coral reefs and tropical fish. You can often see clownfish, parrotfish, and sometimes even sea turtles or rays.
There is no beach here – this place is mainly for enjoying the underwater world. Visitors must follow the rules: no fishing, no stepping on the coral, and no taking marine life. It is a peaceful place for nature lovers and ocean explorers.

What Makes It Special

Marine Park is not just a dive spot – it's a living museum under the sea. Coral gardens stretch wide, teeming with marine creatures. It’s a great place for beginners who want to enjoy slow, relaxing snorkeling. The water is usually calm and visibility is very good. The road to the park is quite narrow and goes through a small village. There are no big shops or cafés nearby, so it's best to buy your food and drinks before the trip. For will see something you must buy tiket on the boat is 350 pesos per person. The location is very close to the ticket office and the boat station — only about 400–500 meters away.

Highlights of Marine Park

  • Underwater Beauty: Colorful corals, schools of fish, and rich marine biodiversity
  • Great for: Snorkeling, free diving, and relaxed scuba sessions
  • Calm Waters: Ideal for beginners and underwater photography
  • Easy Access: Reachable by boat from Coron town in about 20–30 minutes
