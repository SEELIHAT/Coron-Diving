About the Course

The PADI Open Water Diver course is designed to introduce you to the basics of scuba diving. It is the world's most popular scuba diving course, and millions of people have started diving as certified PADI Open Water Divers.

What You Will Learn

  • Scuba diving equipment setup and usage
  • Underwater communication and safety procedures
  • Basic skills for diving in open water conditions
  • Planning and executing actual dives under the supervision of a PADI Instructor

Course Structure

The PADI Open Water Diver course consists of three main phases:

  1. Knowledge Development (Online or Classroom)
  2. Confined Water Dives to practice basic scuba skills
  3. Open Water Dives to review your skills and explore

Course Pricing

The course fee is $550, which includes:

  • All required training materials
  • Use of scuba diving equipment during the course
  • PADI certification fee
