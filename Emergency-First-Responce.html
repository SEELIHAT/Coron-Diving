About the Course

The PADI Emergency First Response (EFR) course equips you with essential first aid and CPR skills. This course is suitable for divers and non-divers alike, providing vital training for handling medical emergencies.

What You Will Learn

  • Primary and Secondary Care techniques
  • CPR and AED usage
  • Handling injuries and illnesses
  • Emergency action planning

Course Structure

The EFR course consists of three main components:

  1. Theory and knowledge development
  2. Hands-on practical exercises
  3. Final scenario-based assessment

Course Pricing

The course fee is $250, which includes:

  • All training materials
  • CPR and first aid practice equipment
  • Certification fee
