General Information About Our PADI Courses

PADI (Professional Association of Diving Instructors) courses are the world's most recognized scuba diving certifications. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced diver looking to advance your skills, we offer the perfect PADI course for you. Our dive center is located in Coron, Palawan. To ensure availability, we recommend booking in advance. Feel free to contact us for more details.

Our Available PADI Courses:

  • PADI Open Water Diver – Learn the basics of scuba diving and earn your first certification.
  • PADI Advanced Open Water Diver – Improve your skills with deep diving, navigation, and more.
  • PADI Rescue Diver – Learn essential rescue techniques to become a safer diver.
  • PADI Divemaster – Take your first step into professional diving.
  • Specialty Courses – Wreck diving, night diving, deep diving, and more.

What’s Included in the Course?

Course fees vary depending on the level. Prices start from 10,000 PHP. Includes: All necessary training materials, full diving equipment, boat trips, PADI-certified instructor, and certification fees.

Why Choose Our PADI Courses?

Our experienced PADI instructors provide high-quality training in a safe and fun environment. Whether you’re just starting or advancing your diving skills, we ensure you gain confidence and certification with the best training methods. Book your PADI course today and take your first step into the underwater world!
