About Port of Coron, Palawan

The Port of Coron is the main ferry terminal where boats arrive and depart from nearby destinations like El Nido, Puerto Princesa, Manila, and Mindoro. It serves as the transportation hub for travelers coming to or leaving Coron by sea. The harbor is located near the town center, just a few minutes by tricycle.

The area around the port is busy with activity: passengers, vendors, tricycles, and sometimes cargo delivery. Ticket offices for various ferry companies are nearby or right at the pier. It's recommended to come 1 hour before your ferry departure. There is a small waiting area with chairs, and some local shops selling snacks and drinks.

What Makes It Important

The Port of Coron is not just a transport hub — it's also the first impression many travelers get of the island. It’s the place where the journey begins or ends, and from here you can head straight to your hotel, diving center, or island tours.

Highlights of the Port

  • Easy Access: Close to town, just 5 minutes by tricycle from the center
  • Connection Point: Boats to El Nido, Manila, Culion, Mindoro, Puerto Princesa
  • Local Vibe: See the daily life of locals and travelers coming in and out
  • Convenient: Nearby ticket booths, small stores, and tricycles ready
