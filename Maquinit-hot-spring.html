About Maquinit Hot Springs

Maquinit Hot Springs is one of the few saltwater hot springs in the world, located just a short drive from Coron town. Surrounded by mangroves and natural rock formations, it offers a unique and soothing experience for travelers seeking relaxation.
The entrance fee is around 200 pesos per person. The water temperature ranges from 38 to 40 degrees Celsius, perfect for soaking after a long day of island hopping or hiking. Wooden walkways and stone pools blend harmoniously with the environment, creating a peaceful and picturesque setting.
"We don't recommend staying in the water for more than 30 to 40 minutes."

What Makes It Special

The hot springs offer a tranquil escape nestled in a mangrove forest, with warm waters and scenic surroundings. It's a favorite spot to relax in the evening as the sun sets over the bay. The saltwater is believed to have therapeutic properties, helping to relieve stress and soothe muscles.

Highlights of Maquinit Hot Springs

  • Natural Setting: Surrounded by mangroves and overlooking the ocean
  • Warm Waters: Naturally heated saltwater pools between 38–40°C
  • Relaxation Spot: Perfect after hiking Mt. Tapyas or island tours
  • Accessibility: About 20 minutes from Coron town by tricycle or motorbaik
