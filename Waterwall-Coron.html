About Seven Falls, Coron Palawan

Seven Falls, also known as the Mini Waterfall, is a hidden gem tucked away in the jungle hills of Coron, Palawan. This peaceful spot is perfect for nature lovers looking to relax and enjoy the cool, clean water. A short and easy hike leads to the waterfall, making it a great escape for families, couples, or solo travelers. There you can swim in the deep but not big lake. But and road to there very beutiful it,s like relaxe for your nervios.

Entrance fee price 50peso, but it's good to leave a donation to the local caretakers who keep the place clean. There's no café or shop nearby, so bring your own water and snacks.

What Makes It Special

Seven Falls is just one waterfall — surrounded by lush jungle. The natural pool is great for swimming or just cooling down your feet. It's quiet, clean, and has that untouched vibe you won't find in touristy places.

Highlights of Seven Falls

  • Natural Jungle: Beautiful green surroundings and fresh air
  • Refreshing Water: Perfect spot to cool off on a hot day
  • Peaceful Vibe: No big crowds, very relaxing
  • Easy Access: Just 25–30 minutes by motorbike from Coron town + 5–10 min walk
