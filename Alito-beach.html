About Alito Beach, Coron Palawan

Alito Beach is a peaceful and less crowded destination in the Coron area, Palawan. Known for its wide shoreline, soft white sand, and clear shallow waters, it's perfect for those seeking relaxation. Entry is 50 peso, and visitors can enjoy a laid-back atmosphere with nature all around. There are no major facilities, so bringing your own mat or towel is recommended. Occasionally, locals offer drinks and snacks. It’s a great place for a quiet picnic, enjoying the breeze, and watching the boats go by."There is a friendly family living on this beach who are happy to treat you to freshly cooked fish - delicious, home-style and inexpensive. And the best part is that the whole beach was just for us: silence, privacy and a real atmosphere of peace made the day unforgettable." "A two-hour scooter ride from Coron hides a real little paradise. But be careful: if you are not confident on a scooter, the last kilometers of the journey can be difficult - there is almost no road. It is especially dangerous to ride after heavy rain."

What Makes It Special

Alito Beach stands out for its peaceful vibes and untouched surroundings. It is not a tourist hotspot, so it's ideal for visitors who want to enjoy Palawan's beauty without crowds. The clear waters are calm and shallow, perfect for wading and snorkeling.

Highlights of Alito Beach

  • Tranquil and quiet location
  • Clear shallow waters for easy swimming
  • Good snorkeling near rocky areas
  • Free access with minimal infrastructure
