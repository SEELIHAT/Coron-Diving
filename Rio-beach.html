Natural Escape in Coron

About Rio Beach, Coron Palawan

Rio Beach is a quiet and naturally beautiful beach located just outside Coron town. It offers a long stretch of sandy coastline, ideal for swimming, sunbathing, or simply enjoying the natural surroundings. There are no crowds and no commercial facilities, which makes it a perfect escape for travelers who love peace and nature. The beach entrance 50 peso to access, and the beach is cleaned, there are places to sit, tables, hammocks, everything is great, We are recommend it , a beautiful tropical beach. It's great for solo travelers, couples, or families wanting to spend a simple day near the water.

Why Visit Rio Beach?

Rio Beach is one of the lesser-known gems near Coron. With no commercial buildings or crowds, it offers real connection to Palawan’s calm environment. Ideal for nature photography, meditating, or just walking along the shore.

Highlights of Rio Beach

  • Unspoiled and uncrowded shore
  • Great for nature lovers
  • Perfect for quiet swimming
  • Free access — bring your own food and mat
