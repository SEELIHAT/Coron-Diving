About the Course

The PADI Junior Open Water course is designed for young adventurers aged 10-14. It provides the knowledge and skills needed to dive safely, making it an ideal introduction to the underwater world.

What Kids Will Learn

  • Basic scuba diving principles
  • How to use scuba equipment
  • Safe diving techniques
  • How to explore the underwater world responsibly

Course Structure

The PADI Junior Open Water course consists of:

  1. Knowledge Development (Online or Classroom)
  2. Confined Water Dives to practice skills
  3. Open Water Dives under supervision

Course Pricing

The course fee is $480, which includes:

  • Training materials
  • Equipment rental
  • Certification fee
