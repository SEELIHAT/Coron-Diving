### Junior Open Water Diver Course (PADI) The **PADI Junior Open Water Diver Course** is the perfect introduction to scuba diving for young adventurers. Designed for kids aged 10 to 14, this globally recognized certification opens the door to the magical underwater world in a safe, fun, and educational way. With guidance from certified instructors, young divers gain confidence and skills that last a lifetime. --- #### **What They’ll Learn** The Junior Open Water course mirrors the adult Open Water Diver program, with adjustments to suit younger learners. It includes three key components: 1. **Knowledge Development** - Learn basic diving principles through engaging materials like videos, manuals, or online resources. - Topics include safe diving practices, underwater communication, and equipment use. 2. **Confined Water Dives** - Practice essential skills in a controlled environment like a pool or shallow water. - Skills include mask clearing, buoyancy control, regulator recovery, and underwater breathing techniques. 3. **Open Water Dives** - Experience four dives in open water under close supervision. - These dives allow young divers to explore the underwater world and apply their skills in real-world settings. --- #### **Why Take the Course?** - **Global Certification**: Junior Open Water Diver certification is recognized worldwide. Once certified, young divers can explore dive sites up to a depth of 12 meters (40 feet) with a certified adult. - **Boost Confidence**: Diving fosters independence and teamwork, helping kids build self-esteem. - **Discover Marine Life**: Learn about underwater ecosystems and the importance of protecting the ocean. --- #### **Course Requirements** - **Age**: 10–14 years. - **Health**: Participants must be in good health and comfortable in the water. A medical questionnaire may be required. - **Swimming Skills**: Basic swimming ability, including a 200-meter swim and a 10-minute float, is required. --- #### **What’s Included** - Training materials tailored for younger learners. - Full scuba equipment, including child-sized masks, fins, and buoyancy devices. - Professional instruction and supervision by certified PADI instructors. - PADI Junior Open Water Diver certification upon successful completion. --- #### **Duration and Cost** - The course typically takes 3–4 days to complete, depending on the schedule. - Costs range from $300 to $500 and usually include equipment rental, training, and certification fees. --- #### **Why Choose Us?** Our dive center specializes in creating a fun, safe, and engaging environment for young divers.
