About the Course

The PADI Repair Diver Course teaches essential skills for diagnosing, maintaining, and repairing scuba equipment. Learn how to ensure your gear functions properly, increasing safety and extending the life of your equipment.

What You Will Learn

  • Basic and advanced equipment maintenance
  • How to diagnose and fix common scuba gear issues
  • Emergency repair techniques
  • Proper cleaning and storage for longevity

Course Structure

The course is divided into three main phases:

  1. Theory: Understanding scuba equipment and troubleshooting
  2. Workshop: Hands-on repair and maintenance training
  3. Practical: Applying skills in real-world scenarios

Course Pricing

The course fee is $500, which includes:

  • All study materials
  • Hands-on repair workshop
  • Certification upon completion
