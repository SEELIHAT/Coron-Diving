About Bintuan Mangrove Park

Explore the Beauty of Mangrove Park Bintu Discover the magic of Mangrove Park Bintuan on a peaceful boat excursion through the serene river surrounded by lush mangrove forest. For just 700 pesos per hour (up to 4 people per boat), you can enjoy a relaxing and romantic journey—perfect for couples or small groups seeking a quiet escape into nat To arrange your trip, simply visit the small shop near the boat station and ask about the boat excursion. They will organize everything for you with ease. Feel the charm of gliding through the water, listening to the sounds of nature, and soaking in the beauty of the mangroves with your loved one by your side. What Makes It Special

Bintuan Mangrove Park is quiet, clean, and feels very close to nature. You can see small crabs and birds, and during sunset, the colors are amazing. Great spot for photos and just walking slowly to relax your mind.

Highlights of Bintuan Mangrove Park

  • Sunset Views: Beautiful light and reflection on water
  • Eco Vibe: No crowds, very quiet and relaxing
  • Close to Coron: About 20–25 minutes by motorbike from town
