Bintuan Mangrove Park

About Bintuan Mangrove Park

Bintuan Mangrove Park is a peaceful nature escape just outside Coron Town. It’s perfect for relaxing walks, birdwatching, and enjoying quiet views of the mangroves during sunset. The wooden paths take you through shallow waters with mangrove trees growing right in the sea. You can come alone or with friends to enjoy the calm vibe and fresh air.

Entrance is around 50 pesos. There is no cafe, so better to bring water and maybe snacks. It’s good for families, couples, or solo travelers who like quiet nature moments.

What Makes It Special

Bintuan Mangrove Park is quiet, clean, and feels very close to nature. You can see small crabs and birds, and during sunset, the colors are amazing. Great spot for photos and just walking slowly to relax your mind.

Highlights of Bintuan Mangrove Park

  • Nature Walks: Wooden path through shallow mangroves
  • Sunset Views: Beautiful light and reflection on water
  • Eco Vibe: No crowds, very quiet and relaxing
  • Close to Coron: About 20–25 minutes by motorbike from town
