How to Get to Coron from Manila or Other Islands?

You can reach Coron (Palawan) by ferry or flight:

By Ferry: Travel from Manila, Puerto Princesa, Cebu, or other islands. It takes around 18 hours. Prices start at 1,800 pesos (depending on class).
Buy ferry ticket at 2Go Travel

By Plane: Flights take about 1 hour 45 minutes, starting at 8,500 pesos.
Buy ticket at Cebu Pacific Air


Buy ticket at Philippine Airlines

How to Get from the Airport to Your Hotel in Coron?

Francisco B. Reyes Airport (Busuanga Airport) is about 30–45 minutes away from Coron town.

• You can take a **shared minivan** directly to your hotel for around 250 pesos per person.
• Vans are waiting at the airport for each arriving flight, no need to book in advance.
• When returning, ask your hotel to arrange your van to the airport — it's quick and easy.

Arriving by Ferry?

If you arrive by ferry at Coron Harbor:

• You can take a **tricycle (tuk-tuk)** to your hotel for around 200 pesos.
• Many hotels also offer **pick-up service** if you ask them while booking.

Travel from El Nido to Coron

• Ferries from El Nido to Coron take about 4.5 hours.
• Some boats offer **WiFi and air-conditioning**.
Buy ticket at Jomalia Shipping

• Other companies offer the same travel time but without WiFi.
Buy ticket at Montenegro Lines
