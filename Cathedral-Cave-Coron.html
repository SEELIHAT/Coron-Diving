About Cathedral Cave

Cathedral Cave is one of Coron’s most unique and memorable dive sites. Located inside a steep limestone cliff, divers enter through an underwater tunnel and emerge into a hidden chamber that opens above the surface. Sunlight beams down from the ceiling, creating a magical “cathedral” light effect.

Why It’s Special

This site is perfect for adventurous divers looking for something different. The mix of darkness, light rays, echoing sounds, and mysterious atmosphere creates an unforgettable spiritual feeling.

Highlights of Cathedral Cave Diving

  • Underwater Entrance Tunnel (starts at ~6 meters depth)
  • Air Pocket Chamber Inside the Mountain
  • Magical Sunlight Rays from Natural Skylight
  • No Waves – Protected by Tall Cliffs
  • Water Temperature up to +40°C in some parts
  • Best for Advanced Open Water Divers or Above

Dive Tips for Cathedral Cave

This is a cave-like environment, so good buoyancy control and comfort in overhead spaces are important. Always dive with a certified guide, bring a dive light, and don’t touch the cave walls. Be sure to ascend slowly when surfacing into the chamber to avoid disturbing the visibility.
