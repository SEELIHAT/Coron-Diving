Plan Your Dive Adventure

Before your dive, visit our dive center in Coron to ensure a seamless experience. We recommend arriving a day early to check your certification, discuss dive plans, and select the best equipment tailored to your needs.

Experience the Dive Day

Our dive day begins at 8:30 AM with equipment checks at our dive center. We then transfer to the harbor and embark on our dive boat, heading towards our dive sites.

A typical day includes exploring historic wrecks and vibrant reefs. After the second dive, enjoy a delicious lunch on the boat amidst serene waters.

We return to the harbor by 5:00 PM, where you can log your dives and plan more adventures in Coron.
