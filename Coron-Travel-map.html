First, decide how you want to get around the island.

The most budget-friendly option is to rent a motorbike, which is available at several rental offices. Alternatively, you can take a tuk-tuk—just negotiate the price directly on the street. It's usually cheaper if you ride with drivers who also transport locals, similar to a shared minibus. A typical round trip to the hot springs (with up to 2 hours of waiting time) costs around 700–800 pesos, and the same goes for a ride to a public beach. You can also Order tours through local travel agencies if you prefer something organized.
