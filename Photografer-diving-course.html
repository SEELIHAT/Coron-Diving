About the Course

The PADI Photographer Diving Course is tailored for photographers who want to enhance their diving skills to capture stunning underwater images. This course provides hands-on experience and techniques to improve your underwater photography.

What You Will Learn

  • Essential underwater photography techniques
  • Use of specialized underwater photography gear
  • Composition and lighting in underwater environments
  • Post-processing tips for underwater images

Course Structure

The PADI Photographer Diving Course includes:

  1. Theory sessions on underwater photography
  2. Practical sessions in controlled underwater environments
  3. Optional sessions for advanced underwater photography techniques

Course Pricing

The course fee is $250, which includes:

  • Instructor-led training in underwater photography
  • Rental of specialized underwater photography equipment
  • PADI Photographer Diving Course certificate
