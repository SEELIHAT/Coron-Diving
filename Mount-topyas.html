Mount Tapyas in Coron

About Mount Tapyas

Mount Tapyas is a prominent mountain overlooking Coron town, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding islands and seascape. The summit can be reached via a stairway of over 700 steps, making it a popular spot for both tourists and locals.
Visitors can enjoy the trek up while experiencing the lush greenery and occasional wildlife sightings along the way. It's an ideal location for watching the sunrise or sunset, providing a serene atmosphere away from the town's hustle and bustle.

What Makes It Special

Mount Tapyas offers an exceptional vantage point for photography enthusiasts and nature lovers alike. It's a refreshing escape into nature, with cool breezes and stunning views that stretch as far as the eye can see. The trek is relatively accessible, providing an opportunity for visitors to engage in light exercise while enjoying the beauty of Coron from above.

Highlights of Mount Tapyas

  • Scenic Views: Panoramic vistas of Coron town, neighboring islands, and the vast ocean
  • Trekking Experience: Over 700 steps leading to the summit
  • Photography Spot: Ideal for capturing sunrise or sunset scenes
  • Natural Beauty: Surrounded by lush greenery and occasional wildlife
