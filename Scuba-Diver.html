About the Course

The PADI Scuba Diver course is your gateway to exploring the underwater world. This course is ideal for beginners and provides the knowledge and skills you need to dive safely and confidently. No prior experience is necessary.

The PADI Scuba Diving Course is perfect for beginners who want to explore the underwater world safely and confidently. This course provides the essential skills and knowledge needed to dive with a buddy under the supervision of a PADI professional.

What You’ll Learn Knowledge Development Understand basic diving principles, equipment use, and safety procedures. Learn through in-person lessons or PADI eLearning. Confined Water Training Practice essential diving skills in a pool or shallow water. Master buoyancy control, mask clearing, and regulator recovery. Open Water Dives Apply your skills in real diving conditions. Enjoy exploring the underwater world while diving under supervision.

What You Will Learn

  • Basic scuba diving principles and safety guidelines
  • How to use scuba equipment
  • Underwater communication techniques
  • Key skills for shallow water diving

Course Structure

The PADI Scuba Diver course consists of three main phases:

  1. Knowledge Development (Online or Classroom)
  2. Confined Water Dives to practice skills
  3. Open Water Dives to apply what you have learned

Course Pricing
