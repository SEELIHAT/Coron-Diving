About the Course

The PADI Scuba Diver course is your gateway to exploring the underwater world. This course is ideal for beginners and provides the knowledge and skills you need to dive safely and confidently. No prior experience is necessary.

What You Will Learn

  • Basic scuba diving principles and safety guidelines
  • How to use scuba equipment
  • Underwater communication techniques
  • Key skills for shallow water diving

Course Structure

The PADI Scuba Diver course consists of three main phases:

  1. Knowledge Development (Online or Classroom)
  2. Confined Water Dives to practice skills
  3. Open Water Dives to apply what you have learned

Course Pricing

The course fee is $450, which includes:

  • All training materials
  • Equipment rental
  • Certification fee
