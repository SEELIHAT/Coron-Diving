About Kilien Falls

Kilien Falls is a peaceful waterfall located in the forest of Coron, Palawan. The journey to the falls includes a light jungle trek, followed by the reward of cool, clear water perfect for swimming and relaxing. The surrounding area is quiet, full of tropical plants and birdsong.

The entrance fee is only 50 pesos. Small cottages are available to rent for sitting and taking a break. Near the entrance, you can find local snacks and cold drinks. It’s a perfect spot for travelers who want to escape the crowds and experience real nature in Coron.

Highlights of Kilien Falls

  • Clean and cool waterfall pool for swimming
  • Short hike through tropical forest
  • Relaxing atmosphere with fresh mountain air
  • Local snacks and drinks available
  • Great for nature photos and peaceful moments
