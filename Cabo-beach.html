About Cabo Beach, Coron Palawan

Cabo Beach, nestled in the pristine waters of Coron Palawan, is a hidden gem for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. Known for its tranquil atmosphere and stunning natural beauty, Cabo Beach offers visitors a secluded retreat surrounded by lush greenery and crystal-clear turquoise waters.
Entrance fee is 50 pesos per person. To lie down, it is better to bring something to lay on the sand. There are bungalows for rent - there is a table inside, a roof, you can sit, but it is not very comfortable for lying down. There is a cafe, a small store and a lot of music on the territory. Prices are about the same as in a cafe in Coron.

What Makes It Special

Cabo Beach enchants visitors with its untouched landscapes and serene ambiance. The beach boasts powdery white sands that stretch along the shoreline, perfect for leisurely strolls and sunbathing under the tropical sun. The surrounding coral reefs teem with vibrant marine life, making it an ideal spot for snorkeling and diving adventures.To see corals and a beautiful underwater world, you need to swim about 200 meters from the shore. Near the beach itself it is shallow - up to 180 cm, but this shallow zone stretches quite far, about 200 meters in length.

Highlights of Cabo Beach

  • Scenic Views: Panoramic vistas of Coron Bay and nearby limestone cliffs
  • Activities: Snorkeling, diving, kayaking, and beach picnics
  • Marine Life: Colorful coral formations, schools of tropical fish, and occasional sightings of sea turtles
  • Accessibility: Accessible by boat and tuktuk or motorbaik from Coron town, offering a peaceful getaway from the bustling city
