Visit Our Dive Office

Before your dive, please visit our dive center in Coron. We highly recommend coming to the office one day before your scheduled dive. This allows us to properly check your diving certification, discuss dive plans, and help you choose the best equipment for your needs. This helps everything go smoothly and safely on the day of the dive.

Equipment Fitting and Certification Check

When you visit in advance, we can prepare everything: wetsuit size, fins, mask, BCD, and tank. You will feel more comfortable and safe underwater when your gear fits well. We also check your certification card (e.g., Open Water, Advanced, Rescue) so we can choose the right dive site for your level.
