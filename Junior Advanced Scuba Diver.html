About the Course

The PADI Junior Advanced Diver Course is designed for young divers who want to take their skills to the next level. This course allows juniors to gain more experience, explore deeper waters, and develop advanced diving techniques in a safe and fun environment.

What You Will Learn

  • Deep diving and navigation techniques
  • Perfecting buoyancy control
  • Exploration of underwater ecosystems
  • Night diving and wreck diving fundamentals

Course Structure

The PADI Junior Advanced Diver Course consists of:

  1. Knowledge Development (Online or Classroom)
  2. Five Adventure Dives focusing on different skills
  3. Open Water Exploration Dives

Course Pricing

The course fee is $350, which includes:

  • All training materials
  • Equipment rental
  • Certification fee
